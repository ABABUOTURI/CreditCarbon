CO2 Tracker and Automated Credit System

A system that helps stakeholders track and manage carbon emissions to receive automatic payments from the government.

Get Started

The Carbon Emission of various industries is stored on the XDC Blockchain with high transparency and stability. The automated credit system helps to reduce carbon emissions.

Our platform is highly efficient for both industry and government. The automated credit system helps to save the earth from CO2 and allows the government to track industry CO2 emissions completely.

AI Interaction

Our system can predict future outcomes based on new input data. The accuracy of predictions depends on the quality of the training data, the chosen algorithm, and the features used.

Accurate predictions rely on high-quality, relevant, and up-to-date historical data. Inaccurate or biased data can lead to unreliable predictions.

Tokenization of Carbon Credits

Transforming Carbon Credit Management with Blockchain

Tokenization, enabled by blockchain technology, is revolutionizing the way carbon credits are managed and traded. It introduces transparency, traceability, and efficiency to traditional carbon credit systems.

Connect XDC Supported Wallet

If you are an industry, connect your industry wallet with our platform. If you are a government entity, connect the respective wallet to access the platform.

CO2 Emission and Credit

The emission of CO2 in the industry is monitored and recorded. Based on the emission, ETH is debited from the industry wallet automatically.

Analysis and Report

Full transparency of the report and the industry spend on CO2 emission. With the industry Wallet ID, anyone can see the report.
